GHANANIAN NAMING SYSTEM.

This is a website where you get your new born a name according to your gender and day that you were born.

In this Akan naming system if you are female you will be named as follows:

  • Sunday: Akosua
  • Monday: Adwoa
  • Tuesday: Abenaa
  • Wednesday: Akua
  • Thursday: Yaa
  • Friday: Afua
  • Saturday: Ama

In this Akan naming system if you were male you will be named as folows:

  • Sunday: Kwasi
  • Monday: Kwadwo
  • Tuesday: Kwabena
  • Wednesday: Kwaku
  • Thursday: Yaw
  • Friday: Kofi
  • Saturday: Kwame
